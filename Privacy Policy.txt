Privacy Policy for MYnager

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Thank you for using MYnager, a café and tea shop management app designed to help business owners manage inventory, employees, sales, and expenses in one place.
Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

MYnager is designed to store and process data locally on your device only.
We do not collect, share, or transmit any personal or business data to external servers.

The information stored locally on your device may include:

User profile data (name, business name, profile photo, bio)

Employee details (name, contact, position)

Product and inventory data

Sales and expense records

Profit and loss reports

Café photos or gallery uploads

App settings and preferences

All such information remains private to you and is not accessible by the developer or any third party.

2. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your mobile device in encrypted form using AES-256 encryption.

No data is sent to any online server or cloud storage by default.

You can optionally back up or restore your data manually using the app’s built-in local backup feature.

The app does not automatically sync or upload data to external servers.

We take appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

3. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions only for functional purposes:

Storage Access: To save reports, photos, and local backups.

Camera Access: To take profile or café photos.

Gallery Access: To upload images for profile and café gallery.

Location Access (Optional): To link your café’s Google Maps listing and view map-based data.

All permissions are used strictly for app functionality and never for external tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

MYnager does not use third-party analytics, ads, or cloud databases.
However, the app may display or open external links to:

Facebook Page

Instagram Page

Google Maps business listing

These links open in your browser or relevant app, and their privacy policies apply separately.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user or business data to any third parties.
All information remains local and under your full control.

6. Your Rights

As the user, you have full control over your data. You can:

View and manage all stored data within the app.

Delete any data or perform a full data reset.

Export and back up your data manually.

Since data never leaves your device, your privacy rights are automatically protected.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect or store information about children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features or legal requirements.
The updated version will be available within the app and will include a revised “Last Updated” date.
